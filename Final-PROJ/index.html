American Muscle Cars

Introduction

Hello, my name is Omar and as a car lover and gear head I have always loved muscle car's for their aesthetics and most importantly their raw power. I would like to share with you my top muscle car choices and why.

The first list, is based upon number's and not aesthetics. Let's compare some number's and power.

Top Five American Muscle Cars

Dodge Challenger SRT Demon 170 (2023)

  • Horsepower: 1,025 hp (on E85 fuel)
  • 0-60 mph: ~1.66 seconds (with drag-prep setup)
  • Top Speed: Around 215 mph
  • Weight: ~4,280 lbs

A drag-strip monster with over 1,000 hp, built purely for jaw-dropping speed.

Chevrolet Corvette ZR1 (2019)

  • Horsepower: 755 hp
  • 0-60 mph: ~2.85 seconds
  • Top Speed: 212 mph
  • Weight: ~3,560 lbs

A high-performance powerhouse that combines luxury and legendary speed in a sleek package.

Dodge Charger Hellcat Red-Eye (2021)

  • Horsepower: 797 hp
  • 0-60 mph: ~3.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: 203 mph
  • Weight: ~4,610 lbs

A four-door muscle car that packs nearly 800 hp, letting you haul friends at insane speeds.

Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 (2020)

  • Horsepower: 760 hp
  • 0-60 mph: ~3.3 seconds
  • Top Speed: 180 mph (electronically limited)
  • Weight: ~4,171 lbs

The ultimate Mustang, with a fierce mix of power and handling for both track and street.

Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 (2022)

  • Horsepower: 650 hp
  • 0-60 mph: ~3.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: ~190 mph
  • Weight: ~3,883 lbs

A hardcore Camaro with track-focused upgrades, ready to tackle corners with muscle and style.

As seen by the number's, this is the reson for the order of my list based on number's and facts.
